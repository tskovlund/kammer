# ADR 0002: AGPLv3 license

## Context

Kammer is privacy-first infrastructure for communities. The main deployment
mode is network service (self-hosted or hosted-for-you). Plain GPL does not
oblige service operators to share modifications.

## Decision

License the codebase under **AGPLv3**.

## Consequences

- Anyone operating a modified Kammer as a service must publish their changes.
- Some corporate contributors will stay away; that is an accepted trade-off
  for keeping improvements in the commons.
- Dual licensing or CLAs are explicitly not planned.
